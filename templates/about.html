About This Project

Welcome to our innovative project for medical insurance cost prediction, incorporating machine learning and built on the Flask framework!. We leverage advanced machine learning algorithms and data analysis techniques to transform the way we estimate medical insurance costs. Our project relies on a robust dataset of medical records, historical insurance claims, and demographic information to provide accurate predictions for insurance premiums. Through Flask, a versatile web framework, we have developed an easy-to-navigate platform that allows individuals and organizations to make well-informed decisions about their healthcare coverage. By offering transparent and data-driven insights into medical insurance costs, we're committed to simplifying the intricacies of insurance, enhancing affordability, and ensuring a healthier, more secure future for all. Join us on this journey towards a more informed and financially responsible approach to healthcare planning.

Dataset Details

Let's explore the dataset used in this project, designed to predict medical insurance costs. It includes key features:

  • Age: Age of the policy's primary beneficiary.
  • Sex: Gender of the insurance contractor.
  • BMI (Body Mass Index): A numeric value calculated from weight and height.
  • Children: Number of children or dependents covered.
  • Smoker: Binary feature indicating smoking status.
  • Region: Residential area categorized into four regions.
  • Charges: The target variable representing individual medical costs billed by health insurance.

This dataset offers valuable insights into how these attributes affect insurance pricing. By building a predictive model, we can estimate insurance charges and gain insights into cost drivers.

The dataset used in this project can be accessed on Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mirichoi0218/insurance
